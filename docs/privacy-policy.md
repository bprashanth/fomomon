# Fomomon: Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 2025

## Information We Collect

### Location Data

- We collect your GPS location to provide location-based services
- Location data is used only for app functionality and is not shared with third parties
- You can disable location permissions in your device settings 

### Camera Data

- We access your camera to capture photos for app features
- Photos are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded unless you choose to share them
- Camera permissions can be revoked in your device settings

### User Account Information

- We collect basic account information when you create a login
- This may include email address and username 
- This account data is only for app functionality and is only stored in encrypted cloud services

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide app features and services
- To improve app performance and user experience
- To communicate with you about app updates 

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for app functionality

## Your Rights

- You can request deletion of your account and associated data
- You can revoke permissions at any time through device settings
- You can contact us with privacy questions or concerns

For data deletion requests, contact: **hello@tech4goodcommunity.com**

For privacy-related questions, contact: **hello@tech4goodcommunity.com**

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes.
